Welcome to Qiubot's Playground!

This is an opensource project for building a robot from the Ballbot family. We would document our component choices, mechanical designs, assembly instructions and also our working progress here. Feel free to give us feedback and help to improve the project.

A bit on the Creators

In the summer of 2016, Cao Chao (@caochao39) and Sun Bacon (@BaconSun) started this project for personal interest. They funded the project themselves (till now). Feel free to give them a hand and donate to the funding! All donators would be invited to play with Qiubot as soon as it is built.
